4 Key Observations

Qwen3-8B: completed 2-epoch training

Completed full training (4,810 steps). Total reward stabilizes around 2.1–2.3 in the second epoch. Consistency reaches ~0.67 with format above 0.94. Rock-stable KL (~0.45) throughout. The strongest controlled learner.

DeepSeek-7B (β=0.01): full recovery

Completed full training (4,810 steps). Recovered from mid-training KL instability (steps 450–650) and reaches total reward ~2.0 with consistency ~0.72 by end of training. Second epoch shows steady improvement.

DeepSeek-7B (β=0): highest reward

Without KL penalty, reaches the highest total reward (~2.8) at step 3,280. Consistency climbs to ~0.79. Shows that removing KL constraint allows faster reward optimization, but may risk reward hacking.

QwQ-32B: steady progress to 740 steps

Extended from 150 to 740 steps. Format compliance rises from near-zero to ~0.85. Total reward reaches ~1.0. Consistency still low (~0.26) but steadily improving. Slowest learner due to model size.

DeepSeek-14B: persistent instability

Both the original config and v4 config show difficulty. Large KL spikes and loss explosions in early training. The original run reaches step 240 with reward still negative (~−0.9). The v4 config with adjusted hyperparameters fares similarly.
